# 📍 MapMates Hub

**MapMates Hub** is the ultimate real-time social navigation platform built to help people connect, explore, and share live locations seamlessly.

🚀 **Live App:** [https://netlify.app](https://netlify.app)

---

## 👑 The Story Behind MapMates Hub (Founder & CEO)

MapMates Hub was independently built by **Faizan Zeeshan**, a 17-year-old student and developer from Pakistan.

### Why I Built This App:
This platform was inspired by a real-life situation. Back when I was in the 6th grade, there was a girl in my neighborhood whom I wanted to talk to, but I couldn't because of the fear and hesitation of living in the same neighborhood (mohallay ka darr). 

I realized that traditional social media requires sharing personal phone numbers or private IDs, which creates a huge barrier and privacy risk for people who just want to connect locally. 

To solve this problem, I created **MapMates**. It is a modern map platform where you can connect, interact, and coordinate with people in real-time **without the need for phone numbers or private IDs**, completely breaking traditional social barriers safely.

---

## ✨ Core Features

* **No Phone Numbers or IDs Required:** Connect and interact safely without revealing your private contact information.
* **Real-time Social Map:** See your community, friends, and live interactions directly on a modern map.
* **Live Location & Tracking:** Share your live coordinates, track destinations, and explore safely.
* **Destiny Missions & Moments:** Share your live status, explore missions, and connect with your neighborhood or campus instantly.
